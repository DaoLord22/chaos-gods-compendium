The Heavenly Node Continent (Terra/Earth)

The world known as the "Heavenly Node Continent" is actually the ancestral home world of humanity - Terra, also called Earth or the Blue Star. Once a thriving martial empire ruled by the Seven Martial Gods, Terra was the center of a galactic civilization that spread the martial path across the universe.

Current State

After the catastrophic invasion and betrayal that destroyed the original martial civilization, the world has been transformed. The green pastures and wheat fields are gone, replaced by a cultivation-focused society that has forgotten its martial origins.

Key Locations

  • Capital of Great Qin: Former martial capital, now the site of great tragedy
  • Limbo Realm: A transitional space for all cultivation paths where mortals shed their shells to ascend
  • Various Kingdoms: Including the ancient Chachapoya Kingdom and Mauretania (creators of the Primal Path)

üìö Lore & History ‚ñ∂

Timeline of Terra's Fall

The Golden Age of Martial Arts

Terra (Earth/Blue Star) thrived under the Seven Martial Gods. The human race lived by the martial code: train youth, defend family, honor ancestors. They ruled and protected their galaxy, spreading the martial path across the universe.

The Arrival of the First Cultivator

A single lost cultivator arrived from another world. The martial artists welcomed him into the Hall of Martial Paths. He spoke of strange distant worlds, selling them "the most dangerous weapon of all‚Äîcuriosity."

The Great Debate

The Seven Martial Gods voted on opening portals to other worlds. Only three Gods (including the Left Commander) voted against it, believing Terra was already perfect. They were outvoted by world governments and the other four Martial Gods.

The Three's Retreat

The three opposing Martial Gods took their legacies and vanished into retreat, wanting no part in what was to come. They remained in seclusion for thousands of years.

Terra's Destruction

When the three Gods finally awakened, Terra was gone. No green pastures, no wheat fields‚Äîjust fire, smoke, corpses, and unimaginable slaughter. The cultivators had committed genocide against humanity.

The Wall of Heads

At the capital of Great Qin, the cultivators built six hundred stone walls covered with human heads, turning the entire city into a monument of death and displaying their victory over the martial artists.

The Great Betrayal

The Storm Fist Martial God, strongest and leader of the Seven, betrayed his own people. He bowed to the cultivators, watched his brothers and sisters be tortured, and ultimately killed the two loyal Martial Gods during their final counterattack.

Zai Zhen's Revelation

Heavenly Supreme Zai Zhen revealed his true purpose: to prove the Dao of Death and Despair by erasing all contradicting paths. Humanity's extinction was necessary for his Dao to become the "only true proven Dao."

The Divine Intervention

After the Left Commander's death, he was summoned to a floating palace by a true divine being. This god fulfilled a pact with a Martial Goddess, extracting the last human blood and sealing it in a Star script to preserve humanity's bloodline.

Present Day

The world now known as "Heavenly Node Continent" has forgotten its martial origins. Xu Yantian, bearer of the preserved bloodline, begins his journey to reclaim the lost martial path and prevent future betrayals.
